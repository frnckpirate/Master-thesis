\subsection{Base flow}
The exercise has a precise base flow. To find it we must find a steady solution to the set of equations :
\begin{flalign}
& \frac{1}{T_o}(U_o^2+V_oU_o') = 1 +\frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T_o}T_o'U_o'+\mu U_o'' \\
%
& \frac{1}{T_o}V_oW_o' = \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T_o}T_o'W_o' \\
%
& U_o-\frac{V_o}{T_o}T_o'+V_o'=0 \\
%
& \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T_o}\frac{1}{Pr}T_o'^2 + \frac{\mu}{Pr}T_o''-\frac{T_o'V_o}{T_o}+(\gamma-1)M^2\muW_o' =0  \\\nonumber
\end{flalign}
